FAQ

Who is The Prepared?
The Prepared is the most-trusted preparedness resource on the web and the leader of the “modern prepper movement” — sane people from all walks of life learning how to prepare in a way that’s based on data, grounded in reality, and easy to implement into modern lives. Our mission is to help regular people prepare for and survive life’s inevitable emergencies, whether it’s common stuff like car accidents and job loss or big events like natural disasters and pandemics.
