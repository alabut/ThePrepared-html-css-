Firecraft

Like Tom Hanks in Castaway, you’ll learn how to make and maintain a fire in unforgiving conditions.

Professional instructors with decades of hard-won experience will teach you everything you know about fire:

  • How to use common emergency tools, including ferro rod fire steels and tinder
  • How to make your own waterproof matches
  • What to look for when you need random natural or man-made fuel sources
  • How to make a ‘primitive’ fire using bow drills (the “rub sticks together” method), the sun, glass, and even ice
  • How to improvise with lessor-known methods, like using a disposable or car battery
